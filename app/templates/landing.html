A process built for premium results

What makes a paint job “expensive” is everything you don’t notice: prep, protection, and clean execution.

1) Protect + Prep

Cover floors, mask surfaces, repair minor defects, scrape/sand, prime, and caulk where needed.

2) Paint with control

Brush/roll/spray based on the surface and finish. Clean edges, even coverage, consistent sheen.

3) Final walkthrough

Touch-ups, cleanup, and a walkthrough to confirm it’s right. You get the “wow” finish.

Want a cleaner look? Ask about modern off-whites, soft sages, and trim refresh packages.
